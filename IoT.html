Internet of Things

What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) connects everyday devices to the internet, enabling them to collect and share data. By embedding sensors and software into objects, IoT allows for real-time monitoring and automation, improving efficiency and decision-making. In smart buildings, IoT helps optimize energy use, enhance security, and improve overall performance through interconnected systems working together.

Mission of the IoT Lab

The IoT Laboratory is dedicated to providing students and industry professionals with hands-on experience in designing, implementing, and managing IoT systems. The lab focuses on equipping participants with the skills to integrate IoT devices, sensors, and networks, enabling smarter solutions in various sectors, including smart buildings. By combining practical training with theoretical knowledge, the laboratory aims to develop professionals capable of addressing the challenges of connectivity, data management, and automation, while driving innovation and efficiency in IoT applications.
