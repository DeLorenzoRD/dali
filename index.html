DALI Lighting Lab

DALI Lighting Lab is a cutting-edge training center focused on advancing lighting technology studies. It specializes in installation, troubleshooting and maintenance, automation design, and real-system implementation of energy-efficient and smart lighting solutions, aiming to cultivate skilled engineers and technicians in the lighting sector of the smart building industry.
