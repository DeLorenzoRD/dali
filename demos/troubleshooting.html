DALI Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting and regular maintenance of the DALI system are crucial steps following commissioning. This involves diagnosing communication errors, identifying and replacing faulty components, recalibrating sensors, and ensuring proper operation of the bus and control devices. Technicians may also perform firmware updates and optimize system configurations as needed. Through hands-on practice, users can develop key skills such as fault diagnosis and resolution, system testing, configuration adjustments, etc.
